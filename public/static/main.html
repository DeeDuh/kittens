About US

Hello! My name is Elena Brodatskaia and I am a breeder of Maine Coon cats. My Maine Coon cattery is called SilverPlace. This name is not without reason, because my heart is forever given to the silver and smoky colors of the Maine Coon. I have been breeding Maine Coon cats since 2014. My rule is that cats should be healthy, happy, surrounded by love and conform to Maine Coon breed standards. I love my cats and I am ready to accept you in our family of Maine Coon lovers
